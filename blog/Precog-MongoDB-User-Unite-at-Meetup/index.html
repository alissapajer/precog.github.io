Precog + MongoDB User Unite at Meetup

Earlier this month, we teamed up with 10gen to launch a Precog implementation of MongoDB. So far, the response has been outstanding and we want to do everything we can to support the users. We’ve added a Precog for MongoDB section to our Developer Center and our support team has been responding to emails, but we want to do more. So, we’ve put together an event in Colorado!

Since we’re fortunate to be located in Boulder, we’d like to take advantage of being surrounded by many developers and the MongoDB Community! Everyone is invited to the first Precog & MongoDB Meetup in Boulder! Here are the details:

Skyrocket your Analytics on MongoDB with Precog

Monday, December 10 at 7pm
Exact location is TBD (somewhere in Boulder)
Sign up now!

Join us for drinks, an interactive dialogue, and to learn more about how to do deep analytics on your existing MongoDB database. We hope to see you there and don’t forget to RSVP and bring your friends for a fun night with the Precog team and MongoDB community!
