There's still time to submit your app! -and get $500-

Precog is partnering with DeveloperWeek to offer a $500 scholarship, DeveloperWeek conference passes, and DeveloperWeek on-stage presentation time for the top developer (or developer team) who proposes an app built on our technology. Submit your app by January 18th (that’s next Friday). Get more info on the scholarship here! 

Here are some app ideas to get you started:

  • Take advantage of Precog’s advanced analytics capabilities to create a recommendation engine

    • Capture data about someone’s favorite book/recipe/programming packages and recommend something new

  • Use the ReportGrid Visualization Library to present data in an informative and interesting way

    • Power a real-time leaderboard for a video game or other competition

    • Use a heatgrid to instantly understand concentrations of activity

DeveloperWeek 2013 Conference & Festival is the first-annual 6-day conference and festival (February 2-7, 2013) in downtown San Francisco where amateur and veteran developers alike can learn about – and build with – over 200 developer tools and languages! DeveloperWeek is a convergence of developers, inviting over 100 speakers to educate you about Node.js, Android, design & UI, Hadoop, MongoDB, and everything new in 2012-2013. We’re honored to be speaking at three sessions and will be exhibiting as well. Hope to see you in San Francisco!
