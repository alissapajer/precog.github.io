You're invited to the Quick Left + Precog Hackfest in Boulder!

We’ve partnered up with our Pearl Street neighbors, Quick Left, to host a Hackfest on January 30. Here’s your chance to show off your mad software development skills, drink cold beer, eat tasty pizza and win some cool prizes. Bonus points awarded to the team who incorporates the ReportGrid API into their web collaboration tool. We’ll be around to help answer any questions and give you some ideas!

Build web collaboration tools in the language of your choice and don’t worry about forming a team beforehand (teams can get together night of). Developers, designers, and spectators are welcome. You’ll have three hours to get busy coding! So come play for a bit. Kicking off at 6:30 sharp. Sign up today!


The super snazzy Quick Left office

When: Wednesday, January 30, 6 – 9:30 pm
Where: Quick Left Headquarters @ 902 Pearl Street, Boulder CO
What: Teams of 3 form at the event to do 3 hours of intensive coding + UXD to build web collaboration tools.
Who: Any and all developers interested in coding, designers in UX/UI, and those who enjoy creating cool shiz under pressure.

It’s a free event so get your ticket here. We hope to see you there and look out for the Precog folks wearing black zombie shirts.
