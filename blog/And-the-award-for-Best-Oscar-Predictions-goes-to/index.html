And the award for Best Oscar Predictions goes to...

The red carpet is rolling out this Sunday night at the 85th Academy Awards. If you're like me, you've been watching the nominated movies to make predictions on who will win. Well, just sit back and enjoy the films and let us do the heavy work on analyzing the data to predict who will be going home with awards!

We've put together Oscars Predictions using our Precog technology to run the analysis and create interactive graphs and charts. In these predictions we're analyzing thousands of Twitter mentions with #Oscars (the official hashtag). Visit our page to see the dynamic charts! Here are the predictions we're making:

  • Best Picture: Using the ReportGrid Bar Graph you'll see which films we predict will win based on positive mentions and reviews. Argo is in the lead right now with Lincoln in a close second!
  • Most Popular Actors/Actresses: This chart shows who is most popular and talked about on Twitter, using our ReportGrid Leaderboard. Jennifer Lawrence from Silver Linings Playbook seems to be the most popular at the moment.
  • Most Social State: Which US state is tweeting about the Oscars? Find out in the ReportGrid Geo Chart. We're not surprised that California has the most buzz!
  • Most Positive Time to Tweet: This ReportGrid Line Graph shows the average sentiment score (using Sentiment Analysis API) by time of day that people are mentioning #Oscars on Twitter.

We teamed up with our friends at AlchemyAPI to use their Sentiment Analysis API to look at positive and negative tweets to predict which movie will win Best Picture. We also used their API to pull all sentiment scores and show when people are tweeting throughout the day.

These charts are in real-time and please check back on the Oscar Predictions page to see how the movies and data is changing! Good luck with your ballots and hope your picks win. Enjoy watching the Oscars this Sunday night and don't forget to see how our predictions line up with the actual results.
