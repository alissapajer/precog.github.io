We're Growing up a Tree!

Summer always seems to fly by, but this one especially has flown by at a supersonic speed and before we knew it the Precog team had grown by six! Since I joined Precog as Director of Marketing a little over a month ago, we added three engineers from across the globe. (I didn't hold the "newbie" badge for very long.) Now I’d like to introduce our newest (and very clever) engineers:

  • Tom Switzer from Toronto, Canada (a Boulderite as of 4 days ago!)
  • Brian McKenna from Brisbane, Australia (soon-to-be a Boulderite!)
  • Erik Asheim from Montreal, Canada (makes the Boulder winters seem mild)

Maybe you’ll meet them online or run into them at a conference (they frequently speak at events) but believe me when I say we have an extremely talented team here at Precog. I am so honored to be a part of this company and very excited for the things to come.
