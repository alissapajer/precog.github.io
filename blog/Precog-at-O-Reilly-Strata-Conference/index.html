Precog at O'Reilly Strata Conference



In case you hadn’t heard, this week is Strata in Santa Clara! The Precog team will be out there participating in a number of different events and we’re excited to be a part of this conference. If you’re attending the Strata Conference Feb 26-28 in Santa Clara CA, be sure to visit our booth P1 in the Innovator’s Pavilion to see how Precog is the Leading Data Science Platform for Developers, and enter to WIN an iPad Mini!

We’re not kidding. We’re seriously giving away an iPad Mini (worth $329) to one winner (randomly chosen) who creates a FREE account with Precog! All entries must be made at Strata and if you’re not able to attend, please tell your friends and colleagues to stop by our booth. Everyone who stops by can pick up a free shirt and if they enter the contest, they’ll receive a Werewolf Game.

Our Founder/CEO, John De Goes, will also be speaking on Thursday. Catch him in these sessions:

The Rise of the Scientific Databases
Thursday 1:30pm – 2:10pm PST
Room: Great America Ballroom J

Office Hour with John De Goes
Thursday 2:20pm – 3:00pm PST
Room: Expo Hall (Table C)

Hope to see you in Santa Clara!
