The Seasons are Changing and so is the Precog Website

Here at the Precog HQ, we've decided to give our website, www.precog.com, a refresh! What does this mean for you? It means that we’ve added new pages and updated our navigation on the site. Now with most company site refreshes, this means re-learning the site navigation and tons of boring new text to read. While we have changed navigation and added new pages, we’ve also added cool graphics to keep you interested. A Precog Zombie on the homepage and action shots of the team to name a few (updated bios and more photos in the coming weeks)!

The new navigation menu is designed to reflect the Precog offerings and also to help get you the information you need – as fast as possible. Need to learn more about what Precog is? Go to Products > Precog. Need the ReportGrid charting solution? Go to Products > ReportGrid. Need to know more about the query language for Labcoat? Go to Products > Labcoat > Quirrel. Not sure what product you need from us? Go to Products to get a description of each.

All companies try to sell products, give appealing/accurate descriptions and make product use easier for their customers. At Precog, we continually do each of these, in addition to offering a one-of-a-kind service which pushes your App and Data Analysis to the next level. Our goal is to provide a great online experience coupled with an outstanding product. In short, to capture your interest, imagination and patronage! So, visit our site, explore the new pages and have fun “hanging out” with the Precog Zombie.
