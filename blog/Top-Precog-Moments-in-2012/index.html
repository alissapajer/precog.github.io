Top Precog Moments in 2012

Happy (almost) New Year! We've put together our most memorable moments from the past year. Thanks for your support and happy holidays from the whole team!

  1. Precog debuts at Strata Conference.
  2. Raised funding from leading big data VC.
  3. Officially launched Labcoat (previously called IDE).
  4. The Precog team doubled in size from 8 to 16.
  5. For the Summer Olympics, we added sample data and queries to Labcoat.
  6. Precog in Public Beta released at DataWeek.
  7. Launched a Real-Time Elections Analysis and partnered with AlchemyAPI.
  8. Added a new blog section written by our Engineers: Precog.Copointed.
  9. Teamed up with 10gen to launch Precog for MongoDB.
  10. Shared our Precog Engineering Challenge with our community.
  11. Launched a new website with use cases, developer center and more.
  12. Celebrated the 12 Days of ReportGrid by sharing our favorite charts.
  13. Attended 12 events around the country and mentioned in over 20 press articles!
