NoSQL Technology & Analytics- Big Data Meetup

We’ve noticed that NoSQL has been a popular topic for discussion lately and creating a buzz in the big data space. That’s why we’re excited about presenting at the March Big Data Meetup next Wednesday, March 13th. You’re We teamed up with the 10gen/MongoDB folks to speak about NoSQL Technology & Analytics. Learn about NoSQL databases and how to perform analytics on your NoSQL data. Join us for a fun evening of engaging presentations, beer, pizza and prizes!

The Google Boulder Office has kindly offered to host our meetup and it will be from 6-9pm. Please RSVP by March 12 to save your spot! Here’s the agenda:

  • 6:00 – 6:30 – Socialize over food and drink
  • 6:30 – 6:45 – Announcements, Upcoming Events
  • 6:45 – 7:30 -
    • Overview of 10gen - Mike Hayes, Sr. Account Executive, 10gen
    • Overview of NoSQL Technology and MongoDB - Antoine Girbal, Principal Engineer, 10gen
  • 7:30 – 8:30
    • Analytics on your NoSQL Data - John A. De Goes, CEO/Found, Precog
    • Overview of Precog for MongoDB - Derek Chek-Becker, Lead Infrastructure Engineer, Precog
  • 8:30 – 9:00 – Door Prizes, Q&A

Hope to see you next week!
