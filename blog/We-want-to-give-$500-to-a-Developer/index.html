We want to give $500 to a Developer!

Precog is attending the DeveloperWeek Conference in San Francisco from February 1st through the 7th. As part of this event, we are also participating in the Scholarship Program and would like to give $500 to a developer who develops an app with Precog (a strong application will propose an interesting use of Precog's API-driven technology).

Submit an application as a team of up to four people by January 18th and make sure to mention "Precog" when asked for the sponsored scholarship. The candidate will then work on the app during DeveloperWeek and will also have a chance to present their app on February 6th at DeveloperWeek!

For an overview of the kinds of solutions Precog offers, check out:

  • Embedded Reporting
  • Data Science
  • Productize Your Data

For resources about how to accomplish these solutions:

  • Developer Center
  • Tutorial Videos
  • Embedded Reporting How-To Guide

Sign-up for a free Precog account today to get your creative juices flowing!

We look forward to reviewing some great applications and don't forget to apply for the Precog Scholarship before January 18th.
